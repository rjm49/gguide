Prefixes

Verb prefixes give a verb a completely different meaning:

come

  • I come from Australia.
  • Caterpillars become butterflies.

take

  • I'll take the red shirt.
  • When a car overtakes, it passes you.
  • We will undertake a dangerous adventure.

However, when you see a verb with a prefix, it will follow the same rules as its "stem" (without the prefix). So:

  • come -> came
    • become -> became
  • take -> took
    • undertake -> undertook
    • overtake -> overtook
